About

Our team was founded in 2017 and is based in Montgomery County, Maryland. This will be our seventh season competing in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC). The team is actually a joint-school team, including members from multiple high schools in Montgomery County such as Poolesville, Quince Orchard, and Northwest High school.

Mission

Our team wants to promote STEM in our community and to introduce new opportunities to younger students! We have been working very hard this year to make in-person opportunities after the COVID-19 pandemic. Check out some of our projects and join us!

BeaverCamps

As a way to promote STEM in our community, our team created and hosted our own summer camps: BeaverCamps. We started this project to help connect students and provide valuable STEM education during and after the COVID-19 pandemic. Click to learn more information if you are interested in joining!
